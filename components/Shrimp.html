Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients Amount
Large shrimp 1 lb.
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Unsalted butter 1/4 cup
Fresh parsley leaves 1/4 cup, chopped
Lemon juice 1 tablespoon
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste
Directions:
  1. In a skillet over medium-high heat, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and cooked through.
  4. Stir in lemon juice and parsley, and cook for another minute.
  5. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
