Chicken Curry

Ingredients Amount
Boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs 1.5 lbs
Onion 1 medium, diced
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Ginger 1 tablespoon, grated
Curry powder 2 tablespoons
Tomato paste 2 tablespoons
Coconut milk 1 can (13.5 oz)
Chicken broth 1 cup
Salt To taste
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Cilantro Optional, for garnish
Directions:
  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt.
  2. In a large pan or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger and sauté for an additional minute.
  5. Add the curry powder and tomato paste and stir to combine.
  6. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and stir to coat in the spice mixture.
  7. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth, and stir to combine.
  8. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pan.
  9. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  10. Serve the chicken curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired, and enjoy with rice or naan bread.
