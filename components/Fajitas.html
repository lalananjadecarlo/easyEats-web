Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients Amount
Chicken breast 2, boneless and skinless
Red bell pepper 1, sliced
Green bell pepper 1, sliced
Yellow onion 1, sliced
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Lime juice 1/4 cup
Cumin 2 teaspoons
Chili powder 2 teaspoons
Garlic powder 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Tortillas 6, flour or corn
Optional toppings:
Avocado 1, sliced
Sour cream 1/2 cup
Salsa 1/2 cup
Cilantro 1/4 cup, chopped
Directions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Heat the oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until browned.
  4. Add the onions and bell peppers to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes or until they start to soften.
  5. Add the garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  7. Serve the fajitas with warm tortillas, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and lime wedges, if desired.
