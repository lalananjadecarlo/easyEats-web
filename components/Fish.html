Baked Salmon

Ingredients Amount
Salmon fillets 4
Garlic powder 1 teaspoon
Paprika 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Lemon 1, sliced
Directions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Sprinkle the spice mixture evenly over the salmon fillets.
  5. Drizzle the olive oil over the salmon fillets.
  6. Top each salmon fillet with a few slices of lemon.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
