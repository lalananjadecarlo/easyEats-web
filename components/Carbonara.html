Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients Amount
Spaghetti 12 oz
Bacon or Pancetta 6 oz, diced
Egg yolks 3
Heavy cream 1/2 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup, grated
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste
Directions:
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water.
  2. In a skillet, cook the bacon or pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from the skillet and place on a paper towel to drain.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the bacon or pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and let it cool for 1-2 minutes. Then, add the egg mixture to the skillet and stir quickly to combine.
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to thin it out. Season with salt and black pepper, to taste.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
